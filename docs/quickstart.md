1. Choose Contests in the bottom nav bar

   ![Contests Tab](../assets/images/tab_contests.png)
2. Press the plus sign Add Contest

   ![Add Contest](../assets/images/add_contest.png)
3. Enter the Contest name and press \<Enter\>

   ![Contests Name](../assets/images/chicken_contest.png)
4. Click on the Contest name to add Question(s) and Answers here:
<p align="left">
<img class="half-image" src="../../assets/images/add_question.png">
</p>
5. Click Add Question, enter the question, e.g. *Which chicken do you like better?*:
<p align="left">
<img class="half-image" src="../../assets/images/chicken_question.png">
</p>
6. Click Add Answer.  Enter some text answers (we'll add images later).
<p align="left">
<img class="half-image" src="../../assets/images/chicken_answers.png">
</p>
7. Scroll down and click the Print/Download section accordion.
<p align="left">
<img class="half-image" src="../../assets/images/chicken_printdl.png">
</p>
8. You can make a PDF or individual PNG files; just scroll down and click Make PDF
<p align="left">
<img class="half-image" src="../../assets/images/chicken_makepdf.png">
</p>
9. Your PDF will be generated showing your Question and Answers.  
<p align="left">
<img class="half-image" src="../../assets/images/chicken_dlprogress.png">
</p>
After it downloads, you can click on the name to print it or display it on your screen.
<p align="left">
<img class="half-image" src="../../assets/images/chicken_dlfinished.png">
</p>

Now you can test your Contest Question by using your phone's camera and scan one of the QR code answers.  When the link appears, click it.  Done!  You're answer will be recorded and you will see the results.
<p align="center" style="width: 90%">
<img width="100%" src="../../assets/images/chicken_pdf.png">
</p>
